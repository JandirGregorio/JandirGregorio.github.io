Instructions

  1. Start by chopping the chicken thighs into bite size cubes. Marinate it with yogourt and spices for one hour (preferebly 24 hrs).
  2. Once your chicken is ready, air fry it for 12 minutes at 380F.
  3. Now the sauce. Heat a pan over medium-high heat. Add the oil, butter, onions, and garlic until lightly golden. Stir ocassionally so it doesn't burn.
  4. Add tomatoes to pan to cook until soft to then add spices and mix.
  5. Transfer your ingredients to blender and blend it up! You can add water to desire sauce consistency.
  6. Transfer sauce back to the pan over slow heat, add heavy cream, and mix through.
  7. Add chicken to the sauce.
  8. Serve and Enjoy!
